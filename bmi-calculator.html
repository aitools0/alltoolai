Kids BMI Calculator & Percentiles

For children and teens (ages 2-19), standard BMI categories do not apply directly. Instead, you must ask: how do you calculate bmi percentile?

Children's BMI is compared against growth charts:

  • Underweight: Less than the 5th percentile.
  • Healthy Weight: 5th percentile to less than the 85th percentile.
  • Overweight: 85th to less than the 95th percentile.
  • Obese: Equal to or greater than the 95th percentile.

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a screening measure that estimates body fat based on height and weight. It helps identify potential health risks related to weight.

BMI formula

  • Metric: BMI = weight (kg) / [height (m)]²
  • Imperial: BMI = 703 × weight (lb) / [height (in)]²

Adult BMI categories

  • Underweight: < 18.5
  • Healthy weight: 18.5–24.9
  • Overweight: 25–29.9
  • Obesity: ≥ 30
